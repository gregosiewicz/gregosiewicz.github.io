About Me

I am an assistant professor at the Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science of the Lublin University of Technology.

Here you can find my profile in Mathematics Genealogy Project.

Research Interest

My research focuses on functional analysis (operator theory, strongly continuous semigroups and cosine functions), and mathematical modeling in biology and physics. Recently, my interests include combinatorics and its connections to computer science.

Popular Science

I am deeply interested in popularizing math and science to a general audience, and encouraging young people who are fascinated by these fields.

See Publications and Talks for my selected popular science papers and slides/videos.

I am also a proud member of the editorial committee of Delta, a Polish popular science journal aimed at high school and university students as well as scientists, which covers topics in mathematics, physics, astronomy, computer science and biology.
